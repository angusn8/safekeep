About SafeKeep

Just face it, you shouldn't use the same basic password for everything on the internet! If you are going to use the same password over and over, at least make it secure. Most people use only basic letters and numbers in their passoword, which are easy for hackers to guess with what is known as a "brute force" attack.

Click on the "passwords" tab of this website to change your current password to something that is as memorable as your old password, and looks just like it, but is way more secure! Of course, you can still get even more secure than this by using different passwords and by encrypting them, but this is a great start!

Disclaimer: SafeKeep cannot fully protect you from being hacked. Please note that passwords are only as secure as you make them and that there are far more advanced methods of securing passwords than this. This website is just to show how you can get a little more secure and still remember your password.
